Quality Control with Computer Vision

Utilizing OpenCV and C++, I made a system that was meant
for usage in small-parts quality control, specifically washers.
The camera would be calibrated against a correct washer and
the number of pixels across a correct washer was would be
inputted into the system via a series of sliders.












Theater Lighting with Global Positioning

Utilizing a Raspberry Pi and a SparkFun GPS-RTK2 sensor,
I attempted to build a system that would take GPS coordinates
and auto-adjust a spotlight based on an actor's position on a
stage. This ultimately ran into issues regarding poor signal
within buildings, but for open-air stages such as concerts
(rather than theater performances), this may still work.
